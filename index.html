Threat Protection

SaaS & Identity Security Posture

As companies expand their digital tools, user identities become key targets for attackers. Canum equips you to:
  • Control access across multiple SaaS platforms
  • Detect abnormal behavior and compromised accounts
  • Evaluate identity-related risk across your entire environment

Security Awareness Training

Your people are your first line of defense but only if they’re prepared. Canum helps organizations:
  • Deliver ongoing, relevant security training across roles
  • Simulate real-world threats to test user readiness
  • Reinforce secure behavior through guidance and insights
We help you turn awareness into action and reduce risk at the human level.

Data Security & Governance

Every data incident starts with human interaction whether it’s accidental sharing, intentional misuse, or account compromise. Canum’s solutions provide:
  • Real-time visibility into how data is accessed, used, and shared
  • Protection against insider threats and unintentional data leaks
  • Policy enforcement that supports regulatory compliance
We help you reduce data risk without slowing down your business.
